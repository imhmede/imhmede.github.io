Welcome

I am an Assistant Professor and Director of Computer Science at Muhlenberg College. I teach computer science. I study AI. How it shapes people, organizations, decisions, and everyday life. My work draws on Science and Technology Studies. On practices. On interactions. On what emerges around AI.

I was born and raised in Mashhad. Eastern Iran. The Khorasan region. As a child, it was simply where I was from. Later, it became something else. A direction. A pull. A way of becoming. I now live in eastern Pennsylvania with my wife. East. But maybe not in origin. That earlier east still shapes me. Quietly. Steadily. From within.

For research, collaborations, or a chat, email me at hamedyaghoobian@muhlenberg.edu. Or visit me in the Trumbower Science Building. Room 125.
